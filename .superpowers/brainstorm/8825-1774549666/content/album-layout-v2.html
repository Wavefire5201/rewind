Key Decisions

  • Timelapse pill — accent border (#8FA67A), visible text. The primary action after "take photo".
  • Import pill — subtle border (#3A3A3E), tertiary text. Available but not competing for attention.
  • Settings sheet — rename, reminders, export, delete. All album management in one place.
  • Export — moved from timelapse screen into settings. Opens the existing format picker (photo album / backup).
  • Timelapse screen — loses its export button. Becomes purely about playback.
  • No more bottom actions or reminders cluttering the album page
